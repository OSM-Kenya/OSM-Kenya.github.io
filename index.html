OSM Kenya

BUILDING A LOCAL OPENSTREETMAP COMMUNITY IN KENYA

About

OSM Kenya is a local mapping community of both individuals and organisations using, contributing, advocating for, and interested in the growth of OpenStreetMap in Kenya.

We would like to extend our deepest thanks to all the volunteers, speakers, and sponsors who contributed to the success of State of the map 2024. OUR MISSION

To create a vibrant community of OpenStreetMap contributors and users in Kenya where members can exchange ideas and support each

OUR VISION

To fully map the country on OpenStreetMap and to strongly advocate for its use within different groups such as government, universities, and organizations.
